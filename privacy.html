Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Unlike other extensions, ChatPin is built on a **Serverless Architecture**.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect your chat logs on our servers. All data captured from AI platforms is stored locally in your browser's **IndexedDB**.

2. Cloud Sync

If you use the Pro sync feature, your data is transmitted directly between your browser and your **personal Google Drive account**. We never see or touch your data.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense for advertisements and Paddle for payment processing. These services may use cookies to track usage for billing and ad targeting.

4. Contact

For any privacy concerns, email us at: offthemain@proton.me
