Common Questions

Is Notion AI really supported?

Absolutely. ChatPin is designed to understand both linear chat flows and document-based AI blocks. You can warp back to specific blocks within a long Notion document just as easily as a chat thread.

Can I change my 5 free slots?

Yes. You can manage and swap your 5 active slots at any time to adapt to your changing AI toolset **(ChatGPT, Claude, Custom Portals, etc.)**.

Where is my data stored?

Privacy is our core philosophy. Your data is stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB. We have no central servers and never see your conversation history.

What is the "Warp" feature?

It's our proprietary navigation logic. We store the unique DOM coordinates of your questions so you can jump back to the exact spot in an infinite chat or document instantly.
